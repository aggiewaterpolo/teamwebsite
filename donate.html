Get Involved

The Texas A&M Men's Water Polo team loves and appreciates everyone involved. Monetary donations help us fund tournaments, buy suits, and keep equipment up‑to‑date.

How to Donate

  1. Go to txamfoundation.com/give.aspx
  2. Select “An Unlisted Account (Enter Manually)” from the “Select a Unit or College” dropdown.
  3. In “Giving Account Name or Number”, enter 942260-99999 and Men's Water Polo.
  4. Enter your donation amount, fill in your details, and complete payment.

Thank you for supporting Aggie Water Polo!
