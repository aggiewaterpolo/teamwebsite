About The Team

The Texas A&M Men's Water Polo team, representing Texas A&M University in College Station, has been a competitive force in collegiate water polo since its inception in the 1950's. The team began to make waves on the club level in 2005, gradually building a reputation for strong performances in regional and national competitions.

With a focus on both athletic excellence and academic achievement, the Aggies have developed a robust program highlighted by conference championships and appearances in national tournaments.

There is a spot for everyone on the team—highly competitive athletes on the A team, moderately competitive athletes on the B team, or a practice squad for those who want to learn and enjoy the sport.
