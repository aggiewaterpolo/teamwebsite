Meet Our Officers

Our officer team is made up of seven highly motivated members of the Texas A&M Men's Water Polo team who were elected to fill their current role. Each one has their own tasks and responsibilities that help the team succeed in and out of the pool.

Xander Zerr — President

Manages the board and external communications.

Xander has been with the team for four years and is passionate about building a strong community both in and out of the pool. He’s the go-to for strategy talks and post-game celebrations.

Mason Mote — Vice President

Organizes trips/events and supports officers.

Mason ensures every away game runs smoothly and is known for his meticulous planning. When he’s not organizing, you’ll find him mentoring new players and keeping team morale high.

JaneDoe — Director of Internal Affairs

Manages YouTube Livestream, Website, and Flywire Estore.

Jane is the tech wizard behind the scenes, ensuring the team’s online presence is professional and engaging. She loves blending creativity with functionality to tell the team’s story.

Parker Kooyman — Treasurer

Manages team finances, refunds, and check requests.

Parker keeps the team’s budget balanced and fundraising efforts on track. His attention to detail ensures every dollar is used to strengthen the program.

Nick Amann — Social Chair

Runs social media, events, and weekly emails.

Nick brings the fun, organizing events that keep the team connected and energized. His creative social media posts capture the spirit of Aggie Water Polo.

Ryan Sharar — Fundraising Chair

Leads fundraisers, sponsorships, and merch.

Ryan is always looking for innovative ways to support the team financially. His energy and networking skills have brought in key sponsorships and partnerships.

Leyton Kraft — Captain

Team captain for 2024–25.

Leyton leads by example in and out of the pool. His dedication to training and team unity inspires everyone to give their best effort.
